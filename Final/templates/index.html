About Smart Irrigo

What is Smart Irrigo?

Smart Irrigo is an AI-powered irrigation monitoring and prediction system that uses real farmland sensor data to forecast energy consumption and optimize irrigation.

It analyzes:

  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Soil Moisture
  • Energy Consumption

The system improves irrigation efficiency, reduces water & energy waste, and supports precision farming.
